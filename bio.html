About



My name is Ulises Gamboa, an artist who likes to draw, build, or create anything. I am from Guadalajara, Mexico and was raised in San Jose, California.
My work is inspired by cartoons, traditional artists and painters, animation, graffiti, and whatever I see though out my day.
I have been drawing since I could remember with my first drawings being of cartoons and later on graffiti and still lives. I want all my art pieces to be fun but technically sound and visually appealing.
My goal is to become a well-known artist with my art being featured in magazines and installations.
